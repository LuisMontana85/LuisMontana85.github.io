About

Luis Rene Montaña Gonzalez

I am a software developer from Mexico. I have a MSc in Advanced Computer Science (Computer Graphics and Virtual Reality) and a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Sheffield, where I am currently working as a postdoc.

I am interested in game development and computer graphics. Most of my work experience is in Unreal Engine 4, but I have also developed in Unity and Godot. I have experience in software development using languages such as C++, C, C#, CUDA, Java and OpenGL.

My PhD work, ‘Sketching for Real-time Control of Crowd Simulations’, focused on developing a sketch-based interface to control crowd simulations in real-time. It was implemented in Unreal Engine and involves game navigation, agent-based modelling, advanced GPU computing and virtual reality. The video below shows an overview of the work.
